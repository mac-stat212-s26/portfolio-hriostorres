Introduction

Hermann’s tortoises inhabit both the island plateau of Lake Prespa and nearby mainland areas in North Macedonia. Island populations are exceptionally dense, and males strongly outnumber females, leading to frequent harassment that affects female body condition. This study examines whether such pressures are reflected in differences in carapace length between sexes across localities.

Research Question

Do female and male tortoises differ in carapace length within each locality?
